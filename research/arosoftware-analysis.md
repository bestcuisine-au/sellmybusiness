## Executive Summary

Arosoftware is a **bootstrapped, privately-held Australian SaaS company** founded in 2005 by James Rubulis, specializing in Real Estate CRM and website design solutions. They serve ~700 agencies across Australia and have expanded into **business broker CRM** with dedicated tools and syndication to 8+ business-specific platforms plus 40+ real estate portals.

**Key Finding:** Arosoftware is a **major syndication hub** for business brokers in Australia, but **no public API for third-party integration** was found. Partnership discussions would likely be required for HospitalityForSale to integrate as a syndication destination.
